What a person gets when they pick their services. Read tomorrow, not today — today is warmed by jobs and inflated by whatever's been browsed; tomorrow is the leading indicator, so a thin number there means a job didn't run and you have a day to fix it.

These days are Eastern time, matching what the app serves and what the Mac's jobs warm. Until 2026-07-16 the server dated the day in UTC, so from 8pm ET it served tomorrow — a day the prewarm had only half-reached. If you ever see the evening go thin again (Spotify especially), check that first: it means config.today_local() fell back to the process clock. Spotify and Apple are always thinnest on a fresh day — they're the keyed resolvers, warmed a capped sip at a time; the keyless ones (Deezer, YouTube, Tidal, Pandora) land near-complete straight away.
